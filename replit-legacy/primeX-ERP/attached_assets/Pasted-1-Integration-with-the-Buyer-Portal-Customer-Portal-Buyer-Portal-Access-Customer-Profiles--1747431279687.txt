1. Integration with the Buyer Portal (Customer Portal):
Buyer Portal Access:
•	Customer Profiles:
o	Each customer in the CRM Module will have a corresponding Buyer Portal account.
o	The Buyer Portal will show the following customer details:
	Order Status
	Inquiries
	Quotations
	Sample Approvals
Customer Permissions:
•	Customers can:
o	View Order Status
o	Approve or Reject Quotations
o	Approve or Reject Samples (e.g., Photo, Fit, PP, Shipping Samples)
o	Approve or Reject Trims/Labels for their orders
________________________________________
2. Inquiry, Order, and Quotation Workflow:
Inquiry & Order Management:
•	Inquiry Tracking:
o	When an inquiry is raised, it should be tracked in the CRM Module and shown in the Buyer Portal.
•	Order Status Updates:
o	When an order is confirmed, the order status should be updated in both the CRM Module and the Buyer Portal.
o	The Buyer Portal should display:
	Order Number
	Style
	Quantity
	Projected Delivery Date
	Order Status (Pending, In Progress, Shipped, etc.)
Quotation Approval Process:
•	Quotation Tracking in CRM:
o	The CRM Module will track all quotations sent to the customer.
o	Customers can view and approve/reject quotations directly from the Buyer Portal.
o	Once approved or rejected, the status of the quotation in the CRM Module should automatically reflect the action.
Sample Approval Workflow:
•	Customers will review and approve/reject the following samples in the Buyer Portal:
o	Photo Sample
o	Fit Sample
o	PP Sample
o	Shipping Sample
•	The approval status will be reflected in the CRM Module.
________________________________________
3. Trims and Label Approval:
Trims and Label Management:
•	Customers will approve or reject trims such as:
o	Labels
o	Hangtags
o	Stickers
•	When trims or labels are sent for approval, they will be displayed for review in the Buyer Portal.
•	Once the customer approves or rejects, the status in the CRM Module should be updated accordingly.
________________________________________
4. Notifications and Alerts:
Customer Notifications:
•	Email/SMS Notifications should be triggered for customers when:
o	A new inquiry or order has been received.
o	A quotation has been sent for approval.
o	A sample has been sent for approval.
o	Trims or labels are pending approval.
________________________________________
5. Order and Inquiry Status in Buyer Portal:
Order and Inquiry Tracking:
•	Customers should be able to track the status of their orders and inquiries in the Buyer Portal with these statuses:
o	Inquiry Status: New, In Progress, Converted to Order
o	Order Status: Pending, In Progress, Shipped, Delivered
o	Quotation Status: Sent, Approved, Rejected, Pending
o	Sample Approval Status: Pending, Approved, Rejected
________________________________________
6. Syncing Buyer Portal with CRM and Order Modules:
Real-Time Syncing:
•	Order Status Updates:
o	Any update to Order Status (e.g., Pending to Shipped) should automatically reflect in the Buyer Portal.
•	Quotation and Sample Approval Workflow:
o	Once a quotation or sample is approved or rejected by the customer, the action should be logged in the CRM Module and automatically updated in the Buyer Portal.
•	Customer Activity Tracking:
o	All actions taken by the customer in the Buyer Portal (approvals, rejections, inquiries) should be logged in the CRM Module.
________________________________________
7. Reporting and Analytics in CRM:
Customer Activity Reports:
•	Sales and Support Teams can generate reports to track:
o	Number of inquiries raised by each customer.
o	Number of quotations sent and their approval status.
o	Sample approvals (number of approved/rejected samples).
o	Order status and performance tracking.
________________________________________
8. Buyer Portal User Interface (UI) Design:
UI Features:
•	The Buyer Portal should have a clean and intuitive interface where customers can:
o	View Orders with order details and status.
o	View and Approve/Reject Quotations.
o	Track Samples and approve/reject them.
o	Approve Trims and Labels.
o	Review and Track Inquiries with real-time updates.
o	Communicate with Sales or Support teams via an integrated messaging system.
________________________________________
Action Requirements:
1.	Integration with CRM and Order Management Module:
o	Integrate the CRM Module and Order Management Module with the Buyer Portal to show real-time updates of orders, quotations, and samples.
2.	Automatic Syncing of Data:
o	Ensure status updates between the CRM, Order Management, and Buyer Portal are synced in real-time, allowing for accurate data display.
3.	Allow Customer Actions:
o	Ensure the Buyer Portal allows customers to approve or reject key aspects like quotations, samples, and trims.
4.	User-Friendliness:
o	Make sure the Buyer Portal is user-friendly, responsive, and secure for customer access.
