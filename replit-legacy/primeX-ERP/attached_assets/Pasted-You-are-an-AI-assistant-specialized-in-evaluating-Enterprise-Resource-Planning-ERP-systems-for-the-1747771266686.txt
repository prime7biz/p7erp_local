You are an AI assistant specialized in evaluating Enterprise Resource Planning (ERP) systems for the garment manufacturing industry.

Our company has developed a SaaS-based, multi-tenant ERP application using Node.js for the backend, React for the frontend, and PostgreSQL for the database. We need to assess its compliance with a comprehensive garment factory workflow, identify gaps, and get suggestions for enhancements.

**Our Target Factory Workflow includes the following core stages and operations (some of which can be in-house or subcontracted):**

1.  **Order Reception & Pre-Production Planning:**
    * Order Intake (Style, Quantity, Sizes, Delivery Date).
    * Tech Pack & Spec Sheet Management.
    * Costing (Yarn, Knitting, Dyeing, Fabric, Printing, Embroidery, Washing, Trims, Labor, Overheads, Subcontracting).
    * Bill of Materials (BOM) Management (Yarn, Fabric, Dyeing, Printing, Embroidery, Trims).
    * Sampling Tracking (Proto, Fit, Size-Set, PPS) & Approvals (Lab Dips, Strike-Offs, Wash Standards).
    * Time & Action (T&A) Calendar Management.

2.  **Material Requirement Planning (MRP) & Procurement:**
    * MRP Calculation for Yarn, Fabric (Greige/Dyed/Printed), Dyes, Chemicals, Printing Inks, Embroidery Threads, Trims.
    * Inventory Check & Purchase Order (PO) Management.
    * Supplier Management.
    * Material Inward & Incoming Quality Control (IQC) for all material types.

3.  **Production Execution & Monitoring (with support for in-house & subcontracting):**
    * **Knitting:** Work Order, Yarn Issuance, Greige Fabric QC & Inventory.
    * **Dyeing:** Work Order, Greige Fabric/Yarn Issuance, Dyed Fabric/Yarn QC & Inventory.

    * **Cutting:** Work Order, Fabric Issuance, Spreading, Marker (conceptually), Cutting, Cut Panel QC, Bundling.
    * **Value Addition on Cut Panels (Embroidery, Placement Print):** Work Order, Panel Issuance, Process QC.
    * **Sewing:** Work Order, Component Issuance, Inline QC, End-of-Line QC.
    * **Washing (Garment Washing):** Work Order, Garment Issuance, Washed Garment QC.
    * **Finishing:** Thread Trimming, Pressing, Final Trims, Final Quality Check (FQC).

4.  **Packing & Dispatch:**
    * Packing (Folding, Tagging, Poly-bagging, Carton Packing).
    * Internal Final Audit.
    * Dispatch Documentation (Packing Lists, Delivery Challans).
    * Finished Goods Inventory Management.

5.  **Subcontractor Management (for any outsourced process):**
    * Subcontract Order Creation & Tracking.
    * Material Issuance & Reconciliation with Subcontractors.
    * QC for Goods Received from Subcontractors.
    * Subcontractor Bill Processing.
